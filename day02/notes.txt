Rock Paper Scissors tournament

input:
- The first column is what your opponent is going to play
    - A for Rock
    - B for Paper
    - C for Scissors
- The second column is what you should play in response
    - X for Rock
    - Y for Paper
    - Z for Scissors

total score is the sum of your scores for each round
score for a single round is the score for the shape you selected plus the score for the outcome of the round
shape scores:
    - 1 for Rock
    - 2 for Paper
    - 3 for Scissors
outcome scores:
    - 0 if you lost
    - 3 if the round was a draw
    - 6 if you won

Part 1
calculate the score you would get if you were to follow the strategy guide


Part 2
the second column says how the round needs to end
    - X means you need to lose
    - Y means you need to end the round in a draw
    - Z means you need to win
